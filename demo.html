What's real in production

  • Leads move through these exact five stages automatically, no manual data entry
  • The Catch asks a quick budget and fit question before a lead enters the full sequence, so leads who can't realistically afford the offer get routed to a lighter nurture track instead of the same high touch effort as a qualified lead
  • Follow Through combines email and a two way AI text conversation, personalized to each lead, not copy paste templates
  • Second Chance adds an AI voice call on top of that, so a no-show gets a real conversation offering to rebook, not just another message
  • Every email includes a working unsubscribe link, honored automatically, no chasing someone until they block you
  • Discovery calls book directly on your real calendar
  • This view pulls from your own Google Sheet, no new login or platform to learn

What's simulated for this demo

  • All names, dollar values, and notes below are fictional sample data
  • The "Simulate a new lead" button compresses a process that normally unfolds over days into a few seconds
  • Your real dashboard reflects your actual leads and offer price, not these placeholders
  • SMS and AI voice call consent, carrier registration, and legal review are handled before any of this goes live for you
